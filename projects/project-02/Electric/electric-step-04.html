Step 4: Loop and Bend

<
>

Loop

Take the slack you gave yourself and wrap it around the outside of your tuning machine. It should fall over top of the portion of string that is still sticking out the other side.

Bend

Create tension by pulling down the portion you just wrapped. Bend the excess string sticking out of your tuning machine so that it is pointing up.
