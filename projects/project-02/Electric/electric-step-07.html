Step 7: Trim and Tune

<

Trim Excess

Clip your strings as close to your wrap as you can. Be careful not to accidentally clip one of your new strings.

Tune

Tune your guitar accordingly. It will take a while for the strings to stretch out and set, so you will have to tune it over the course of a day or two before it stays.
