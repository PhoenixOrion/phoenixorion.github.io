Step 2: Threading the Strings

Align Tuning Machines

Make sure all of your tuning machines are turned so that the holes are at a slight angle with respect to the neck of your guitar.

Thread Strings Through Back

Slide your strings through the backside of your guitar, making sure you have the gauges in the right order from heaviest to lightest.
