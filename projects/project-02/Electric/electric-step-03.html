Step 3: Through the Tuning Machine

<
>

Through Inside to Out

Make sure all of your tuning machines are turned so that the holes are at a slight angle with respect to the neck of your guitar.

Thread Strings Through Back

Pull the string back through the hole about an inch to maybe an inch and a half to give yourself some slack. A good rule of thumb is roughly the space between two tuning machines on your headstock.
