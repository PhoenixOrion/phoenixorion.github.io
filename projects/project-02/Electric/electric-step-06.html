Over-Under

Repeat using this same method; many people refer to this as the “over-under” method. The first wrap looping over top of your excess string, and the following wraps falling underneath, creates a lock on the string.

Work Inside to Out

Continue changing your strings from the inside of the guitar to the outside. Do not clip of the excess until the very end in case you make a mistake.
