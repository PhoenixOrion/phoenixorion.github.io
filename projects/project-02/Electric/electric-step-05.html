Step 5: Winding Strings

<
>

Maintain Tension

Maintain tension by pressing the string down with your index finger, and pulling the rest of the string up with your other fingers.

Tighten

Wind the string so that it rests on the inside of the headstock. Your tension should be maintained so that these following wraps wind underneath the portion sticking up.
